The platform aims to enhance storytelling experiences by transforming written storylines into engaging video clips. By leveraging user prompts, the website will generate short video clips that vividly depict the events of children's books, making understanding and enjoyment more accessible and captivating. More specifically, in this system, a user will initially browse for video that’s already existing within the system or from their history of videos stored. If they wish to create a new video for a new prompt, they basically enter the storyline and wait for the video to be displayed.

Assumptions:
• User Engagement: The success of the platform assumes that users, particularly children, will be engaged and interested in the video book content provided.
• Parental Supervision: It is assumed that parents or guardians will supervise and guide children's interactions with the platform, ensuring they access age-appropriate content and use the platform safely.
• Technology Adoption: Users are assumed to have access to the necessary technology (e.g., internet connection, devices) to use the platform effectively.
• Educational Value: The platform assumes that video books have educational value and can contribute to children's literacy, creativity, and cognitive development.
